What is a Hair Transplant?

A hair transplant is a permanent surgical solution for hair loss. It involves extracting healthy hair follicles from a donor area (usually the back of the head) and implanting them into areas experiencing thinning or baldness.

At Decent Aesthetics Clinic, we utilize the latest microscopic technology to ensure 100% natural-looking hairlines with maximum density and zero visible scarring.

Our Methods

FUE Method

Follicular Unit Extraction is the gold standard in hair restoration. Individual follicles are extracted one by one.

  • No linear scarring
  • Faster recovery time
  • Natural distribution

FUT Method

Follicular Unit Transplantation allows for the highest yield of grafts in a single session.

  • Maximum graft count
  • Ideal for advanced hair loss
  • High survival rate of follicles

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the procedure painful?

No, the procedure is performed under local anesthesia. You may feel a slight prick initially, but the actual surgery is completely painless. Patients often watch TV or sleep during the treatment.

When will I see results?

New hair growth typically begins after 3 to 4 months. By 6 months, you'll see significant improvement, and final results are visible 12 to 15 months post-procedure.

Do you offer Beard & Eyebrow Transplants?

Yes, we specialize in facial hair restoration using the same advanced micro-FUE technique for completely natural-looking beards, mustaches, and eyebrows.
